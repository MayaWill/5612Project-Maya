Project Homepage

This project investigates the relationships among Type 2 Diabetes, Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), and Heart Disease through the analysis of blood biomarkers and machine learning techniques. By comparing biomarker patterns across these conditions, the project seeks to identify shared risk factors and evaluate whether CKD may serve as an intermediate condition linking diabetes and cardiovascular disease.
